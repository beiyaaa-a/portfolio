My Works
So here in the first image is one of my fav drawings! This is Satoru Gojo from JJK. I drew him using just a lineart pen. The reference is from his famous meme “Nah, I’d win.” I had fun drawing this one since I didn’t have to look for a color palette — the manga panel was already in black and white!
This is Revy from Black Lagoon. I personally don’t know this character — I drew it as a gift for someone! This piece is actually one of the biggest I’ve ever done, and it was also my first time using a canvas and acrylic paint. Since it was my first try, it’s kind of a mess of course, but it was such a fun learning experience, and I’m still proud of how it turned out.
This guy right here is Roronoa Zoro from one of the peak anime, One Piece. I just drew him because — why not? I wasn’t really aiming for anything specific, I just wanted to try drawing him in color.
This is a drawing I did of Aomine Daiki from Kuroko no Basket – he's definitely one of my top characters in the show! This piece was actually a gift for a friend who's also a big fan of KNB.
